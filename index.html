Welcome to Manan
Vidya Manrakhan
Mahto School

“Manan Vidya Manrakhan Mahto School” came into existence on 22nd April, 2007 to meet the growing need of a good co-educational English Medium School keeping high standards in Ranchi. The institution follows “Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE)” curriculum. It imparts structured and purposeful education to the young children. It is a foundation school for many coveted professions and careers.

The school is situated in a tranquil environment by the side of NH-33 at Ranchi, the capital of Jharkhand. Such an environment helps to nourish the inner peace which is an essential ingredient for study and creativity. Every effort is being made by the school to take the students on a voyage of discovery of Indian traditions and heritage.
